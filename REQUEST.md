## Description
Module for receiving a 433MHz RF signal from a settable remote control. The signal causes the activation of an output.


## Specifications

* Voltage: 3.5V-12V
* Ampere: max 1mA
* Outputs: 1
* Package: any SMD
* Form factor: smallest form factor
* Work frequency: 433MHz
* Encoding: PT2262
